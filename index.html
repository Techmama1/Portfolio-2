About Me!

I have an entreprenurial spirit and enjoy learning new things. Currently, I am attending the Full-Stack Coding Bootcamp at University of Texas in Austin, with a completion date of October 9, 2018. Before attending Bootcamp, I utilized my MBA and culinary degrees to start my own bakery. I have also worked as a Sous Chef for a large catering event center. Additionally, I am a proud Navy Veteran, who spent my enlistment as an Aircrewman who was trained as an Aviation Electricians Mate (AE). After my enlistment, I worked as a Civlian Contractor for 6 1/2 years. I believe that these diverse experiences were of great value. All of my collective experiences have led me to the belief that, "every problem presents an opportunity". Because of this, I want to be part of an organization that takes obstacles and works through them in a multi-faceted way in order to come up with the best solution, all the while, allowing the solution to be continually impressed upon with new and unique ideas.
